About Jinx

Jinx is a bespoke coffeehouse in central Binghamton, New York. We offer a variety of espresso and other coffee-based drinks as well as delicious baked goods made in-house. We hope you'll enjoy the calming, comfortable atmosphere and stay a while. The world can be a stressful place, and sometimes you just need to escape. When you do, we'll be here.

Our Product

We offer high-end coffee drinks including custom lattes, teas, and other chilled beverages. We also offer house-made baked goods like crepes, petit fours, and cookies.

Our Approach

Too many coffee joints try to get you in and rush you back out. We want you to sit in, stay a while, maybe even read a book or get some work done. We're attempting to return "the coffeehouse" to a place people want to be, instead of a place they quickly stop by.

Our Goal

We want to be a place of peace and respite from the outside world, supplying incredible coffee and delicious baked goods, and a nice place to sit and enjoy them instead of rushing to your next destination. We want you feel welcome, and feel unburdened by everything back out in the real world.

Our Mission

We wish to exude the feeling of a "Third Place", a place that is not home or work where you can commune with others of similar or different upbringing, economic condition, race, religion, etc. We wish to be a place that we think is missing from the world, where people come together to improve not only themselves, but the world around them. We want you to know: you are welcome here.
